Slip And Slide

A game about making it to the end and discovering the world ahead of you. Making it further into the game causes disruptions and the worlds become more and more unpredicatable and aggressive. The player will be forced to make decisions that could cause death and game over. Luckily there's a helpful world merchant eager to sell you some goods and helpful items in enchange for some precious materials. This game has been rather fun to work on, programming and designing the maps and story for this has taught me quite a bit.
