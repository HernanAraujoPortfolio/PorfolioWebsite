Welcome to my portfolio, I hope to share enough information about myself and also display my love and passion for game development. As a self taught developer I've dabbled in several developing professions, but specialize in game programming and design. I was a UI Layout for a mobile app called "Zoo Country" on IOS (picture bellow on the right). After this I focused on self teaching, creating projects and laying out ideas. I then moved to Vancouver to pursue my career, where I worked at Electronic Arts and successfully helped launch Fifa 20. I held ownership/lead the Volta Stability Team as a "Development Support" this was a very new and exciting game mode for Fifa20 and it was an honour to have a great part in it.
